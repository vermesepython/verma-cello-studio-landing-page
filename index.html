Meet Your Instructor

Welcome to the Verma Cello Studio! My name is Arjun Verma, and I started this private studio to support beginner cellists of all backgrounds. Whether you are an adult picking up the instrument for the very first time, a middle schooler preparing for school orchestra, or anyone in between, you will find a supportive and structured learning environment here.

I am an experienced cellist who has performed extensively across multiple symphony and chamber orchestras with a passion for making people passionate. I am incredibly grateful to the people who taught me how to express myself through the most objectively beautiful instrument out there, and I want to unlock that joy in others by giving them the fundamental skills to do so.
