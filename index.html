Meet Your Instructor

Welcome to the Verma Cello Studio! Founded and taught by Arjun Verma, this private studio is dedicated entirely to beginner cellists of all backgrounds. Whether you are an adult picking up the instrument for the very first time, a middle schooler preparing for school orchestra, or anyone in between, you will find a supportive and structured learning environment here.

Arjun is an experienced cellist who has performed extensively across multiple symphony and chamber orchestras. His teaching approach focuses on building a relaxed physical technique, developing a beautiful tone, and making the journey of learning the cello completely approachable and rewarding.
